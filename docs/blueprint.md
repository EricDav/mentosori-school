# **App Name**: CampusConnect

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Image Gallery: Showcase school life and events through a rotating image gallery.
- About Us Section: Provide detailed information about the school, its mission, and its staff.
- Interactive Contact Form: Enable users to send inquiries and messages directly to the school administration.
- Secure Online Registration: Allow parents/students to securely submit their applications online.
- News and Announcements: Update students and parents with the latest school news and announcements.
- AI-Powered Content Tool: LLM 'tool' analyzes current school events to suggest relevant news articles.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calming Navy Blue (#30475E), evoking trust and stability.
- Background color: Clean White (#FFFFFF), provides a crisp and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant Pink (#E45858), used sparingly to draw attention to key interactive elements.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif for clear and accessible body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a modern sans-serif to capture the user attention.
- Use flat, modern icons to represent different sections and functionalities, enhancing usability.
- Employ a clean and structured layout with ample white space to enhance readability and reduce visual clutter.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and provide visual feedback on interactions.
